Service Policy

🌟 Introduction

Welcome to Gido Bot! We are dedicated to providing a safe, enjoyable experience for all users. Please review our service policies to ensure a smooth and secure interaction with our platform.

🛡️ User Responsibilities

Maintaining a respectful and compliant environment is essential. As a user, you agree to:

  • ✅ Adhere to Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • 🚫 Avoid using the bot for any illegal activities or spamming.
  • 🤝 Foster respectful conversations with other users.
  • 🔒 Keep your account credentials safe and confidential.

⚙️ Bot Functionality

Gido Bot provides a wide range of features, including moderation tools and interactive commands to enhance your experience on Discord. For a full list of functionalities, please refer to our user guide.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. We handle your data with the utmost care. We only save the Channel IDs, Server IDs, Admin IDs, and Role IDs, and we ensure that these IDs are 100% secure. Only the bot developers can access them.

📅 Service Availability

We strive to ensure Gido Bot is available 24/7. However, there may be occasional downtimes due to maintenance or technical issues. We appreciate your understanding and patience during such times.

✏️ Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update or modify this service policy as needed. Any significant changes will be communicated through our Discord server and other relevant channels.

📞 Contact Us

If you have any questions or require assistance, feel free to reach out to us via our Discord Server. We're here to help!
